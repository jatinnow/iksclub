Conference Themes

Domains of Inquiry

Five interconnected streams spanning India's classical scientific tradition.

🔭

Astronomy & Mathematics

Kerala School, Vedāṅga Jyotiṣa, Siddhāntic astronomy, and proto-calculus.

⚗️

Chemistry, Metallurgy & Materials

Rasaśāstra, Bhasmā medicines, spectroscopic validation of Hiṅgula & Haritāla.

🌿

Āyurveda & Life Sciences

Personalised medicine, Vrikshāyurveda, Herbal Kuṇapajala bio-fertilizers.

🏛️

Architecture & Sacred Geometry

Temple building technology, spatial proportion, ritual design and cosmology.

📜

Scientific Language & Grammar

Vyākaraṇa, Vedic Śikṣā, Sanskrit as a vehicle for knowledge transmission.
