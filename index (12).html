Who We Are

The IISER Pune IKS Club

The IISER Pune Indian Knowledge System (IKS) Club is a scholarly community at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Pune, dedicated to the interdisciplinary study of India's rich scientific and technological heritage.

India (Bharat) has been known for its rich civilizational heritage for millennia. The broad term Indian Knowledge System encompasses Vedic sciences, mathematics, astronomy, chemistry and metallurgy, Āyurveda, civil engineering, architecture, yoga, classical arts, and much more — all sub-disciplines with centuries of treatises as testimony.

We bring together faculty, research scholars, and students to bridge this ancient wisdom with contemporary scientific inquiry, in alignment with the National Education Policy 2020.

Our Vision

To create a lasting institutional home for interdisciplinary scientific dialogue at the intersection of ancient Indian knowledge and modern science — right here at IISER Pune.

The traditional, holistic approach of IKS and the reductionist methods of modern science together offer a powerful platform for building a resilient, equitable future with a moral order.

Our Focus Areas

IKS Disciplines We Explore

From the cosmos to the chemistry of ancient medicines, our club spans the full breadth of India's scientific tradition.

🔭

Astronomy & Mathematics

Exploring the Kerala School, Vedāṅga Jyotiṣa, Siddhāntic astronomy, and the contributions of Āryabhaṭa, Bhāskarācārya, and Mādhava — including their proto-calculus and quasi-heliocentric planetary models.

⚗️

Chemistry, Metallurgy & Materials

Investigating Rasaśāstra — the ancient science of chemistry and metallurgy — the use of metals in Bhasmā medicines, and spectroscopic validation of traditional substances like Hiṅgula and Haritāla.

🌿

Āyurveda & Life Sciences

Understanding personalized medicine from Āyurvedic traditions, Vrikshāyurveda (the science of plants), Herbal Kunapajala bio-fertilizers, and their modern scientific validation.

🏛️

Architecture & Sacred Geometry

Examining temple architecture as a living technology — integrating ritual, spatial proportion, and energy design informed by cosmology, consciousness, and metaphysical principles.

📜

Language, Grammar & Epistemology

Studying Vyākaraṇa (scientific grammar), the Vedic Science of Śikṣā, Sanskrit as a vehicle for knowledge transmission, and Indian philosophical epistemological frameworks.

🎓

IKS Integration in Education

Promoting IKS integration into modern academic curricula per NEP 2020, through course design, pedagogy workshops, and outreach for students and faculty alike.

Club Activities & Programmes

Annual National Conference

Our flagship event — the National Conference on IKS: Science & Technology (NCIKSST) — brings leading scholars and students together for a full day of lectures, panel discussions, and poster presentations.

Regular reading circles on classical IKS texts — from Yuktibhāṣā and Āryabhaṭīya to Rasaratnākara — alongside faculty-led seminars connecting ancient methodologies with current research.
